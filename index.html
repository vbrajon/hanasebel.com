Hana Sebel Portrait Painter

Hana Šebelová explores the duality in the portrait, the deposited psychology of intimacy and reality that surrounds it. Based on the portrait away from the notion of artistic genre to conceive as container conceptions of identity.
Pablo Villaizán
As far as I remember I felt a passion for portraits, attracted by eyes that are speaking their own story. The look speaks about things that we can contemplate emotionally but can't analyze or describe with words. This is exactly the space that I like to explore... going behind the curtain of consciousness and transmit the mystery of the soul.
